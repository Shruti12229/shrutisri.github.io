Our Story

Demo Site was created as a lightweight sandbox to help validate web crawler behavior. It contains multiple linked pages with realistic metadata so that crawl results can be easily verified.

Why a Demo Site?

  1. To verify that a crawler discovers all pages
  2. To check that titles and descriptions are parsed correctly
  3. To confirm internal links are followed
  4. To test edge cases like duplicate content or redirects
